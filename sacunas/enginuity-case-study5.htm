Problem:
Dentsply wanted to move a production line to York, PA and increase its throughput. The deadline and budget were tight and they needed a contractor that would assure them the schedule would be met.

Solution:
Dentsply met with Enginuity to present the project requirements. Within two weeks, Enginuity returned with a preliminary budget and various alternatives to mitigate expenses. An agreement was reached, and Enginuity’s team of architects, designers and contractors worked with the owner to polish the design and complete the construction within the owner’s timeframe.
