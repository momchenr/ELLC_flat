Turbocor

Enginuity is proud to be central Pennsylvania's exclusive certified retrofit contractor of Turbocor, a breakthrough in compressor technology. This friction-less, magnetic-bearing compressor is perfect as a retro fit solution or as an element in a new design/build. Turbocor’s reliable and simple new technology (the only moving part is magnetically levitated and is decoupled from any other internal component) can replace your air-cooled or water-cooled centrifugal and roll top applications.

There are documented energy efficiency savings of 30–50% with the Turbocor, and because it is oil-less, you can expect up to a 50% reduction in maintenance costs as well. Other advantages include integrated controls, a quiet 77db, and it is half the size and one fifth the weight of other compressors.
